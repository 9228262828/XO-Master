  Privacy Policy for CalcPro
  Last updated: March 2026

  CalcPro ("the App") is a free, offline calculator application developed
  by Ahmed Shiref ("the Developer"). This Privacy Policy explains how the
  App handles information.

  1. Data Collection
     CalcPro does NOT collect, transmit, or store any personal data on
     external servers. All account information (username, email, hashed
     password) is stored exclusively on the user's own device using
     Android's local storage (SharedPreferences).

  2. Data Storage
     - User credentials are stored locally on the device only.
     - Passwords are hashed using SHA-256 and are never stored in plain text.
     - No data is sent to the Developer or any third party at any time.

  3. Permissions
     CalcPro requests no device permissions. It does not access the
     internet, camera, contacts, location, microphone, or any other
     sensitive device resource.

  4. Third-Party Services
     CalcPro does not use any third-party analytics, advertising,
     or crash reporting services.

  5. Children's Privacy
     CalcPro does not knowingly collect data from children under 13.
     Because the App collects no data at all, it is safe for all ages.

  6. Changes to This Policy
     If this policy is updated, the new version will be published at
     the same URL and the "last updated" date will change.

  7. Contact

STEP-BY-STEP UPLOAD GUIDE
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

  1.  Go to https://play.google.com/console
  2.  Sign in with your Google account.
  3.  Click "Create app" (top right).
  4.  Fill in:
        App name:          CalcPro - Calculator
        Default language:  English (United States)
        App or game:       App
        Free or paid:      Free
      → Tick both policy checkboxes → Click "Create app"

  5.  LEFT MENU: Grow ▸ Store presence ▸ Main store listing
      → Paste short description, full description
      → Upload icon_512x512.png as the App icon
      → Upload feature_graphic_1024x500.png as the Feature graphic
      → Upload the 4 screenshots in the "Phone" section
      → Fill Contact email

  6.  LEFT MENU: App content ▸ Privacy policy
      → Paste the privacy policy URL (from GitHub Gist or your website)

  7.  LEFT MENU: App content ▸ Content rating
      → Click "Start questionnaire" → choose "Utility" → answer all No
      → Click "Save" — you will receive the EVERYONE (E) rating

  8.  LEFT MENU: App content ▸ Ads
      → Select "No, my app does not contain ads"

  9.  LEFT MENU: App content ▸ App access
      → If all features are accessible without login: "All or most features
        accessible" — BUT since your app requires login, select
        "All or most features are restricted" and add instructions:
          Username: testuser
          Password: test123
        (First register this account on your device, then note it here)

  10. LEFT MENU: Release ▸ Production ▸ Create new release
      → Upload:  build/app/outputs/bundle/release/app-release.aab
      → Release name:  1.0.0 (1)
      → Paste the "What's new" text from Section 8 above
      → Click "Review release" → "Start rollout to production"

  That's it — Google will review the app (usually 1–3 days for first
  submission) and then publish it on the Play Store.
