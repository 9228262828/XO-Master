Introduction

SpendWise ("the App", "we", "us", or "our") is a personal expense-tracking application developed for Android. This Privacy Policy explains how the App handles information when you use it.

By installing or using SpendWise, you acknowledge that you have read and understood this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with any part of this policy, please do not use the App.

2

Information We Do NOT Collect

SpendWise is a fully offline application. We do not collect, transmit, store on servers, or share any of the following:

  • Personal identifiers (name, email address, phone number)
  • Financial account numbers, bank details, or card information
  • Device identifiers (IMEI, Android ID, advertising ID)
  • Location data (GPS or approximate location)
  • Usage statistics, analytics, or behavioural data
  • Crash reports or diagnostic data sent to external servers
  • Any data that identifies you personally

✅ We do not collect, store, or share any personal information through SpendWise.

3

Data You Enter into the App

All data you enter — expense amounts, categories, dates, notes, and settings — is stored exclusively on your device using a local database (Hive). This data:

  • Is never uploaded to any server or cloud service
  • Is never shared with us or any third party
  • Remains entirely under your control at all times
  • Is permanently deleted when you uninstall the App
  • May be included in your device's system backup (if enabled by you)

You own your data. We have no access to it whatsoever.

4

App Permissions

SpendWise requests only the permissions necessary to deliver its features. Below is a complete list of every permission the App uses and why:

Permission Purpose Required?
POST_NOTIFICATIONS Display daily expense reminder notifications Optional — only requested if you enable reminders in Settings
RECEIVE_BOOT_COMPLETED Reschedule your reminder notification after the device restarts so you don't miss it Only active if reminders are enabled
SCHEDULE_EXACT_ALARM
USE_EXACT_ALARM		 Deliver reminder notifications at the exact time you choose Only active if reminders are enabled

The App does not request camera, microphone, contacts, location, storage read/write, or any other sensitive permission.

5

Third-Party Services

SpendWise does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. Specifically, the App does not include:

  • Google Analytics or Firebase Analytics
  • Facebook SDK or any social media SDK
  • Advertising networks (AdMob, Meta Ads, etc.)
  • Crash reporting services (Crashlytics, Sentry, etc.)
  • Any service that transmits your data to an external party

Google Fonts (Typography only): On the very first launch, the App may make a network request to download the Poppins font from Google's servers. This request contains no personal data — it is a standard HTTP request for a font file. After the first download, the font is cached locally and no further requests are made. This request is governed by Google's Privacy Policy.

6

Local Notifications

If you choose to enable daily expense reminders in Settings, the App uses your device's local notification system (flutter_local_notifications) to schedule reminders. All of the following is true:

  • Notifications are generated and delivered entirely on your device
  • No notification content is sent to any external server
  • You can disable reminders at any time in Settings
  • You can revoke notification permission via your device's system settings
7

Data Export (CSV)

SpendWise includes a feature to export your expense data as a CSV file. When you use this feature:

  • The CSV file is generated locally on your device
  • You choose where to share or save the file using your device's share sheet
  • SpendWise has no access to or visibility into where you send the file

Once exported, the file is under your sole control. If you share it with a third-party service, that service's own privacy policy will apply.

8

Data Security

Your data is protected by your device's built-in security (screen lock, encryption). Because SpendWise stores all data locally and transmits none of it, the risk of a data breach from our side is zero — we simply have no access to your data.

We recommend using a screen lock and keeping your device's operating system up to date to protect all apps on your device, including SpendWise.

9

Children's Privacy

SpendWise is not directed at children under the age of 13. The App does not knowingly collect any information from children under 13. Since the App collects no personal information from anyone, it is safe for use by people of all ages. If you believe a child has somehow provided personal information through the App, please contact us at the address below and we will take appropriate action.

10

Your Rights and Control

Because all data is stored locally on your device, you have full control at all times:

  • Access: Open the App to view all your data
  • Correction: Edit any expense or category directly in the App
  • Deletion: Delete individual items inside the App, or uninstall to delete everything
  • Portability: Export all your data to a CSV file from Settings → Export Data
  • Restrict processing: Disable notifications at any time from Settings

No request to us is needed — you are the sole owner and controller of your data.

11

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in the App or applicable laws. When we make changes, we will:

  • Update the "Effective date" at the top of this page
  • Release a new version of the App on Google Play
  • Note the change in the App's release notes (changelog)

We encourage you to review this page periodically. Continued use of the App after any changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy. Because we do not collect your contact information, we cannot notify you directly of changes.
