Privacy Policy

SpendWise is built with a privacy-first approach. All your data stays on your device — we never collect, sell, or share your personal information.

Last updated: March 2025  •  Version 1.0
✅ 100% Offline  •  No data collection

1. Information We Collect

SpendWise does not collect any personal information. The app operates entirely offline. All data is stored locally on your device and never transmitted to any server.

  • No names, emails, or contact information
  • No financial account numbers or banking data
  • No location data
  • No device identifiers or analytics data
  • No usage statistics or behavioral data

2. How Your Data Is Used

All data you enter into SpendWise is used solely to provide the app's core functionality:

  • Displaying your expense history and summaries
  • Generating charts and reports
  • Storing your preferences (theme, currency, language)
  • Scheduling local notifications (reminders) on your device

This data never leaves your device. We have no access to it.

3. Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored locally using Hive, a lightweight and secure NoSQL database, in your app's private sandboxed storage. Only SpendWise can access this data. Uninstalling the app permanently deletes all your data.

4. Third-Party Services

SpendWise does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. No advertising SDKs, no analytics platforms, no social media integrations. The only network activity may be loading the Poppins font from Google Fonts on first launch — no personal data is sent.

5. Notifications

Daily reminders are generated entirely on your device using the local notification system. No external server is involved. You can disable reminders at any time in Settings.

6. Data Export

The CSV export feature generates a file on your device. You control where it goes via your device's share sheet. SpendWise has no visibility into where you send the exported file.

7. Children's Privacy

SpendWise is not directed at children under 13. Since we collect no information at all, the app is safe for use by people of all ages.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will update the "Last updated" date above and release a new app version for material changes.

9. Your Rights

You have complete control over your data:

  • Access: Open the app to view all your data
  • Correction: Edit any expense or category directly in the app
  • Deletion: Delete items individually or uninstall the app
  • Portability: Export your data to CSV from Settings
